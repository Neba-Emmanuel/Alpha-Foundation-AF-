About Us

Welcome to Alpha Foundation

The Mission of Alpha Foundation is to bring to the lime light the identity of the less privileged, identify and expose young talented persons, transform the society via training and entrepreneurial skills. Above all, fight against gender base violence to both men and women.



Who are We?


ALPHA FOUNDATION(AF), is a non-profit making organisation based in Kumba, South West Region of Cameroon. Out of the Zeal to educate and train young Cameroonains, We have been laying down alot of emphasis on Gender Base Violence, Education for all, Relief and Rehabilitation, Capacity development and Entrepreneurship. So far within these few years since 2015, we have brought great ideas to existence, that which we use today as our back born for training in the following domains; Tailoring, Hair dressing, Craftsmanship for women(making of Beads and Facinators), perfect training Ms Office and production of good Motivation Letter's and CV's. We have been able to allocate jobs via our Career Development Orientation Scheme.
However, we look forward to do more of this only via your commitment and donations.

Aims


To provide provisions, support and Empowerment Opportunities to the lost in the Society at large; the displaced, the orphans and capacity building for youths.

Objectives


Alpha Foundation(AF) will fulfill the aim by:

  • Promoting and fostering humanitarian support to the less privileged in the society.
  • promote entrepreneurship ideas among members.
  • carry out training in diverse fields of capacity building.
  • Promoting talent enhancement support Program.
  • To raise funds and receive contributions where appropriate to finance the work.
  • Organize meetings, training courses and events.
  • Work with similar groups and exchange information and ideas with them.
  • Take any action that is lawful, which would help it to fulfill its aims.
