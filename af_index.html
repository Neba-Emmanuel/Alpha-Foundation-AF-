Preamable

This foundation is the brain-child of the Mr ITOE HENRY ITOE. Its inception can be dated as far back as 2007, when he was still a student in the University of Yaounde I. Here he was the student president of the OROKO student, resident in Yaounde. Disagreements,tribalism and other forms of malice amn the Oroko students particularly and elites generally, prompted Mr ITOE to work for the community. He decided to bring together all the Oroko sudents despite their ethnicity, counsel many on the ills about tribalism and preached hardwork to them. When calm returned the endeavor never died down. He then diverted to Humaniterian work to the less privileged, orphans, gender based violence(GBV) and entrepreneurship for aspiring youths. He single handedly sponsored this endeavor that gave him a tick from all as sundry. The program continued in kumba with same zeal when he got employment with NEXTTEL. Here, he was working with more than two hundred people, from different works of life. He was a team leader, measuring rod, and a do as I do leader who proved negativity to positivity in every domain when things turned to slow down...

However, in the year 2015,he and some of his team members decided to come up with this draft piece of work which is going to help bind members and make functioning very official. Thus, this constitution is based on the principles of the Christian Bible with inspiration from
  • The Holy Spirit.
  • It sets principles which shall guide and govern the foundation in the guise of reaching the lost in the various communities in south West and North West regions particularly and the entire Country as a whole.
  • Under the protective cover of the universal declaration of Human Rights as adopted and enshrined in the constitution of the Republic of Cameroon, Alpha Foundation claims and ascribes to herself independent and exclusive in carrying out its humanitarian activities in the country, discipline, general organization, functioning and the right and prerogatives to lawfully, alter, amend and modify her constitution and to take and implement decision on matters concerning the Lost, the displaced, orphans and youths.

Aims

To provide provisions, support and Empowerment Opportunities to the lost in the Society at large; the displaced, the orphans and capacity building for youths.

Objectives


Alpha Foundation(AF) will fulfill the aim by:

  • Promoting and fostering humanitarian support to the less privileged in the society.
  • promote entrepreneurship ideas among members.
  • carry out training in diverse fields of capacity building.
  • Promoting talent enhancement support Program.
  • To raise funds and receive contributions where appropriate to finance the work.
  • Organize meetings, training courses and events.
  • Work with similar groups and exchange information and ideas with them.
  • Take any action that is lawful, which would help it to fulfill its aims.

We love to provide provision, support and create empowerment opportunities to the lost in the society.
